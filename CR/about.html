Sound & Movement

Music is the sound of the soul, and Hypelab is here to feed the soul. Based in Phnom Penh, Hypelab is an underground music group who has dedicated their talents to invest in the future of the music and event scene of Cambodia. In 2018, 7 memeber came together to discuss and collaborate on a scene they felt were missing from around the country. They started in an apartment room and as of today have expanded to their own club called Black Phnom Penh. Now Hypelab provides services from video production to providing entertainment.
